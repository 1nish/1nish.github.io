Overview

In the summer of 2019, I joined ThoughtSTEM as a software development intern where I wrote code to build games that would be taught to students. I also had the chance to work at the summer camps and teach students how to code using an in-house language built with Dr.Racket. Seeing kids grasp the concepts of coding at such a young age was gratifying and priceless. After this experience, I strongly believe that coding should be made into a mandatory subject in primary school curriculums.
