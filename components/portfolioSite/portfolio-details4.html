Paragon: Project Trashion's Second Runway Show (2019)

Project Trashion is a group funded by the University of Waterloo that aims to promote sustainability in the most fashioinable ways. As a Thrift Stylist, for their second annual runway show, I had the role of styling a 4-piece collection on models consisting of second-hand clothing that was sourced from thrift stores. Getting to meet people within my school that were as obsessed about clothing as I was, as well as collaborating with other creative minds and seeing the show being built and perfectly executed was an experience that I enjoyed and took a lot from from.

From collecting luxury pieces, since a young kid, to being able to put collections together was a true joy and dream come true.
