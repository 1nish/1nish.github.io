Project: Personal Portfolio

The first 4 photos above are prototypes that were designed using Adobe Xd. My idea for this project was to create a digital portfolio resembling a Manila folder. This idea came to me as I was brainstorming for personal portfolio website themes - I wanted to present something that showcases my design abilities as well as my unique style of creative expression. Thinking back to times before the tech era, portfolios and resumes were submitted in physical form. My goal was to emulate this concept with a modern, digital twist, paying homage to the designers of the previous generations. Developing the site took lots tweaking and updating to ensure the best experience, and is still in need of updating as it isn't yet responsive.
